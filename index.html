Layout

The suggested maximum page width is 1020px, but go wider if the content requires it. This will help maintain a good line length which will help with reading.

Design for smaller desktop screens and mobile devices first, this will help keep focus on the page and ensure users of all devices are able to access content. When designing a page, use white space and appropriate levels of headings to create a visual hierarchy and structure so users can follow content in a consistent way and aren’t interrupted.

Header and Footer

It’s important to maintain consistency across the organisation’s digital manifestations so users visiting any of the local Citizens Advice websites are able to interact intuitively and with confidence. The header area should consistent of a logo and, where applicable, any contact information. The search box is an optional integration, it should be placed on the right hand side of the header in place of any contact details and should be limited to searching your site only.
